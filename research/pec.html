Fabrication of a flexible photo-electrochemical cell using laser processing

Hyeonjae Kim, Heejung Kong, and Junyeob Yeo

We demonstrate a method to fabricate flexible photo-electrochemical (PEC) cells by using femtosecond laser-induced reduction of graphene oxide...

The processed material retains its flexibility and conductivity, showing promise for wearable PEC devices and low-cost production.
